Frecuencia central de banda

Freq. Central
Fc = Finf * sqrt(2)
Finf = Fc / sqrt(2)

Fsup = F * 2^n
Finf = F * 2^(1/3)

Ejemplo
31.5
Fsup = F * 2^(1/3)
Finf = Fc / sqrt(2)
= 31.5 * 2^(1/3) = 39.68
= 31.5 / sqrt(2) = 22.27
