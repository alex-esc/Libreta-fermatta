División de tercios de octava superior e inferior

(tercios de Octava) [octava]
Finf = Fc / (2^(1/3))  Finf = Fc / (2^(1/2))

Fsup = Fc * (2^(1/3))  Fsup = Fc * (2^(1/2))

ejemplo 1k ire, sup 3 de Oct
Finf = 1000 / (2^(1/3)) = 793.7 Hz
Fsup = 1000 * (2^(1/3)) = 1,259.9 Hz
